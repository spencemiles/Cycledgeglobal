About CycleEdge

Africa's fastest growing digital cycling community and resource hub

Your Complete Cycling Ecosystem

CycleEdge is a dynamic and innovative cycling platform developed to unite, empower, and elevate the cycling community across Africa. Established in early 2024, CycleEdge was created to provide a centralized digital space for cyclists, vendors, tour organizers, and enthusiasts to interact, share resources, access services, and engage with the growing cycling culture in Africa.

At its core, CycleEdge brings together everything that matters in the world of cycling—from curated cycling events and guided tours, to gear reviews, exclusive product deals, local vendor support, and global community engagement. Our platform serves as a bridge between cycling enthusiasts and the services they need, while promoting cycling as a sustainable mode of transportation, a competitive sport, and a lifestyle choice.

Meet Our Founder: Philemon Mutisya

CycleEdge was founded by Philemon Mutisya, a passionate cyclist and tech entrepreneur who recognized the need for a unified platform to serve Africa's growing cycling community. As an active member of the local cycling scene, Philemon has seen firsthand the challenges faced by cyclists and cycling businesses.

His vision was to create a digital ecosystem that would connect all aspects of the cycling world - from professional riders to weekend enthusiasts, from small bike shops to major manufacturers. What began as a simple idea to help local cyclists find group rides has grown into Africa's most comprehensive cycling platform.

"Cycling transformed my life, and I wanted to create something that would make cycling more accessible, safer, and more rewarding for everyone across Africa. CycleEdge is more than a business - it's a movement to put Africa on the global cycling map."

Under Philemon's leadership, CycleEdge has grown from a small Nairobi-based startup to a pan-African platform serving cyclists in 12 countries. His unique combination of cycling expertise and tech innovation continues to drive the company's vision forward.

Our Vision & Mission

Driving the cycling revolution in Africa

Our Mission

To empower every cyclist in Africa with the tools, resources, and community they need to thrive. We believe cycling can transform lives—through improved health, eco-friendly transport, and community empowerment. Our mission is to break down barriers to cycling access across the continent.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted and vibrant online cycling hub—where cyclists of all levels can connect, discover, and grow, while driving social, economic, and environmental impact through two wheels. We envision a future where cycling is a mainstream transportation and lifestyle choice across Africa.

Our Direction

We're growing into an all-in-one platform to connect African cyclists globally, promote local businesses, educate riders, sustain cycling culture, and engage youth through clubs and sponsorships. Our roadmap includes expanding to 30 African countries by 2026 and launching a cycling academy in 2025.

Our Achievements

Milestones in our journey to transform cycling in Africa

2024 Q1

Platform Launch

Successfully launched the CycleEdge platform with initial partnerships with 5 local bike shops and 3 cycling clubs in Nairobi. Our beta version attracted 1,000 users in the first month, validating the need for a unified cycling platform in Africa.

2024 Q2

First Major Event

Hosted the Nairobi Challenge with over 200 participants, marking our first major cycling event promotion. The success of this event led to partnerships with 15 additional cycling clubs across Kenya and Tanzania.

2024 Q3

5,000 Users

Reached our first major milestone of 5,000 active users across Kenya within just 6 months of operation. Launched our vendor marketplace feature, enabling 50+ local bike shops and mechanics to list their services online.

2024 Q4

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to Uganda, Rwanda, and South Africa, growing our user base to 10,000+ cyclists. Partnered with 12 major cycling events across East Africa, becoming the official registration and promotion platform.

2025 Q1

Brand Partnerships

Created exclusive deals with international brands to provide cycling gear and bikes at subsidized costs for our community. Launched our mobile app with route tracking, event notifications, and in-app messaging features.
