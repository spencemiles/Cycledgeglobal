About CycleEdge

Africa's fastest growing digital cycling community and resource hub

Your Complete Cycling Ecosystem

CycleEdge is a dynamic and innovative cycling platform developed to unite, empower, and elevate the cycling community in Kenya and beyond. Established in early 2024, CycleEdge was created to provide a centralized digital space for cyclists, vendors, tour organizers, and enthusiasts to interact, share resources, access services, and engage with the growing cycling culture in Africa.

At its core, CycleEdge brings together everything that matters in the world of cycling—from curated cycling events and guided tours, to gear reviews, exclusive product deals, local vendor support, and global community engagement.

Our Vision & Mission

Driving the cycling revolution in Africa

Our Mission

To empower every cyclist in Africa with the tools, resources, and community they need to thrive. We believe cycling can transform lives—through improved health, eco-friendly transport, and community empowerment.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted and vibrant online cycling hub—where cyclists of all levels can connect, discover, and grow, while driving social, economic, and environmental impact through two wheels.

Our Direction

We're growing into an all-in-one platform to connect African cyclists globally, promote local businesses, educate riders, sustain cycling culture, and engage youth through clubs and sponsorships.
