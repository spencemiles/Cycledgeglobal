Benefits of Cycling

Health Benefits

Boosts cardiovascular fitness, strengthens muscles, burns calories, and reduces risk of chronic diseases.

Mental Benefits

Releases endorphins, improves sleep and focus, reduces stress, anxiety, and depression.

Environmental

Zero emissions, reduces traffic congestion and noise pollution, promotes eco-tourism.

Economic

Cheaper than cars, low maintenance, supports local bike businesses and tourism.

Social

Builds community through group rides, encourages inclusivity across all ages and backgrounds.

Cycling as a Hobby

Anywhere!

Cycling is one of the most accessible and rewarding hobbies you can take up. It requires minimal equipment to start and offers numerous benefits:

  • Improves physical and mental health
  • Environmentally friendly transportation
  • Social connections through group rides
  • Explore your local area in new ways
  • Can be as relaxed or competitive as you want

Start with short rides and gradually increase distance as your fitness improves. Join a local club to meet other cyclists and learn routes.

Tour de France & Top Cyclists

Tour de France: The Ultimate Cycling Challenge

The Tour de France is the most prestigious and challenging cycling race in the world. Held annually in July, it covers approximately 3,500 kilometers (2,200 miles) over 21 days of racing.

Recent Winners:

  • 2023: Jonas Vingegaard (Denmark)
  • 2022: Jonas Vingegaard (Denmark)
  • 2021: Tadej Pogačar (Slovenia)
  • 2020: Tadej Pogačar (Slovenia)

Top Current Cyclists:

Tadej Pogačar

Slovenia | UAE Team Emirates

2x Tour de France winner, known for his climbing and time trial abilities.

Jonas Vingegaard

Denmark | Team Jumbo-Visma

2x Tour de France winner, exceptional climber with strong team support.

Wout van Aert

Belgium | Team Jumbo-Visma

Versatile rider excelling in classics, time trials, and sprint finishes.

Kenyan Cyclists Making Waves:

While Kenya hasn't yet produced a Tour de France rider, cyclists like Suleiman Kangangi (RIP) and John Njoroge have represented Kenya in international competitions like the Tour of Rwanda. The future looks bright with development programs like Safari Simbaz nurturing young talent.
